What We do?

At Nett Hands, we understand the level of trust and dedication that comes with entrusting the care of a loved one to another. Understanding that great honor we are provided all services with an unwavering dedication to ensuring the health, safety and quality of life of our clients, while exhibiting compassion, respect, integrity and ethical practice.

Intermittent Home Care

Whether you are coping with an illness, need help after a hospitalization, or find it difficult to leave your own home you deserve the very best possible care. Home care can assist with your recovery to your optimal state of well being. Home visits are scheduled according to need and may range from daily to once a month. Each visit typically last about one hour or less.

  • Wound Care
  • Blood Draws
  • Diabetic Evaluation
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
  • IV Hydration
  • IV Infusion Therapy

Non-Medical Home Care

When an individual is no longer able to independently sustain his or her lifestyle without additional help, non-medical home care supports clients with their daily routine, allowing clients to live in the comfort of their own home safely, while safeguarding independence.

  • Escort to Appointments
  • Errands
  • Shopping
  • Companionship
  • Personal Care
