About us

A Little Help, Makes a Big Difference

Who we are?

At Nett Hands we use the team approach which guarantees the best specialty care for each patient. The team may include skilled nurses, physical therapists, occupational therapists, medical social workers, speech therapists, and home health aides. Our staff delivers care under the supervision of the patient's primary physician and develops an individualized plan of care.

How We Work?

We carefully screen our staff with extensive background checks, social security validation, and diligent reference checks.

All staff are fully skilled, licensed, and or certified to administer professional health and/or social services.

Accepted Insurance & Payors

  • Medicare
  • Medicaid
  • Private Insurance
  • Self-Pay
  • Managed Care Organizations(MCO)
